Our Unique Story

Welcome to Air 034 Club, a one-of-a-kind establishment nestled in the heart of Kitengela, Kajiado, Kenya. Our unique club is built inside a massive Boeing airplane, offering an experience unlike any other.

What started as an ambitious vision has transformed into a premier destination for those seeking luxury, adventure, and unforgettable memories. From our famous Choma meat to our executive club experience, we've created a space where every detail matters.

Located just outside Nairobi, Air034 provides the perfect escape from the city hustle while remaining easily accessible. Come experience the magic of aviation-inspired hospitality.

Choma & Dining Experience

Famous Kitengela Choma

Our specialty is authentic Kenyan Choma - expertly grilled meat that's tender, flavorful, and cooked to perfection. Using traditional techniques and the finest ingredients, our chefs create a culinary experience that keeps guests coming back for more.

From succulent nyama choma to grilled specialties featuring the best of Kenyan produce, our menu celebrates local flavors with a touch of elegance.

Signature Dishes

  • Traditional Beef Choma
  • Grilled Goat Meat
  • Chicken BBQ Special
